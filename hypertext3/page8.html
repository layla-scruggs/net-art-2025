Now what?

Unsure, you submit to whatever this cave has planned for you.
Directionless, again.
Still, you walk and walk and walk.
You know you've exited the cave now.

You are urged forward, backward,
Left turn,
Left turn,
Right turn.

Maybe years pass.
You aren't sure.

Somehow, you knew exactly where you would end up.
Layers of cloth now obstruct your vision, though you don't remember tying them around your head.

You are reunited with the mouth of the cave,
and then the inverted snowman,
and then the vault door.

Something's different.

You remove the first cloth,
then the second,
then the third.
